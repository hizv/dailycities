Welcome to the /daily/ website!

What is Daily?

/daily/ is an online community of friends who share a common passion: discovering and listening to new music. Kickstarted as the "Daily Album Challenge" on July 1st, 2014, by GivingBear, /daily/ has since outgrown the original "a new album a day" idea and become a place to share music and other artforms. In early 2019 the /daily/ Discord Server was founded and that is where most of us now communicate with each other.

This website was an old dream of having a community blog or website where we could post our articles, reviews, and other music-related musings. Through it, we hope to spread awareness about lesser-known artists and bands we love. Welcome to /daily/, let's find some tunes!
